The html() method is used to get the content of the selected element, including the heml markup.

<p>
    jQuery is <b>fun</b> fun
</p>
<p>
    jQuery is <b>fun</b> fun
</p>
//jQuery
$(function() {
    var val = $('p').html();
    console.log(val);
});
//outputs: jQuery is <b>fun</b>
